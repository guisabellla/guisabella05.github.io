Dear Honored Guest,

Welcome to the glorious Republic of Cats—a land where feline wisdom reigns, naps are sacred, and humans exist primarily as food dispensers and professional belly rubbers (on an extremely conditional basis).

As you step into our esteemed republic, please familiarize yourself with our sacred laws:

All furniture belongs to the cats. Sit at your own risk. Any unclaimed lap is hereby considered occupied.

Food must be served promptly. Delays in meal service may result in excessive meowing, judgmental glares, and the occasional paw slap.

Doors shall remain open. Any closed door is an act of treason. Should you open it, we reserve the right to not enter, stare at you blankly, then walk away.

Nap Time is Law. If a cat is sleeping on your work laptop, you are legally required to cease all productivity and admire their majesty.

All boxes are property of the Cat Republic. They shall be claimed and occupied immediately, regardless of size, shape, or intended use.

The Human Citizens' Duty: Your role in this republic is vital—providing treats, belly rubs (when requested), and acting as a warm, stationary surface for lounging. Do not question your purpose. Embrace it.

Punishments for Noncompliance: Penalties may include dramatic tail flicks, midnight zoomies, passive-aggressive furniture scratching, and an elaborate hairball conspiracy against your favorite rug.

We welcome you with open paws and expect your full devotion to the Supreme Order of the Feline Overlords. May your days be filled with purrs, playtime, and the ever-present feeling that you are being silently judged.

Enjoy your stay, humble servant—I mean, honored guest!

Sincerely,
The High Council of Whiskers
